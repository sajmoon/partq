PartyQ Social queuing

beta

Spotify queueing for parties made Simple.

For the host

Open the PartyQ app in Spotify. Choose a hashtag. Watch your guests queue up songs for you. No more broken queues or songs. No registration required.

For the guest

Fire up Spotify mobile. Share your track to Twitter. Add the designated hashtag to your tweet. Your track will be added to the queue instantly. No registration required. No downloads required.

Available in Spotify App Finder soon.
